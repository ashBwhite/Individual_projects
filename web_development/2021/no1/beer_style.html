Stout and Porters

Intensely dark coloured beer. Has deep, roasty flavours of chocolate and coffee.

Oatmeal Stout and Porter

Made with oatmeal instead of barley or malt. Oatmeal gives thicker body and silky mouth feel and adds nutty and earthy flavours. Pairs good with raw oysters.

Imperial Stout and Porter

Originated from Russia/Baltic region. Originally brewed in late 18th century England to export across Baltic sea. Pairs good with sweet cakes or chocolate desserts.

Special Beers

Beers with special ingredients to add flavours to lagers or ales.

Fruit Beer

Lime, strawberry and cherry are most common ingredients to add. Most of them are sweet and pairs great with desserts.

Honey Beer

Honey beers have cooper-coloured beers with creamy mouth feel and caramel like flavours.

Canadian Brewery Explorer

The Canadian Brewing Award(CBAC) is Canada's national competition for judging the quality of Canadian manufactured beer. They give award by beer type as well as nominates best beer of the year.
